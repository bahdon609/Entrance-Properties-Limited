Welcome to Entrance Properties Limited, a trusted name in the construction industry of Bangladesh. Since our founding, we have been committed to delivering high-quality construction services that reflect our dedication to excellence, honesty, and long-term value. As a growing company in an ever-evolving sector, we are proud to contribute to the infrastructure and urban development of our nation. Established in the early 2000s, Entrance Properties Limited was formed with a simple but powerful vision — to build a better future. From the beginning, our goal has been to create buildings and spaces that are not only structurally sound but also serve the needs and aspirations of those who use them. Over the years, we have successfully completed a wide range of projects, including residential apartments, commercial complexes, office spaces, and industrial facilities. Our strength lies in our people. We are backed by a highly experienced team of engineers, architects, project managers, and construction workers who share a common passion for quality and innovation. Our professionals use proven construction practices, careful planning, and the latest tools and technologies available at the time to ensure that every project is completed on schedule, within budget, and to the satisfaction of our clients.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, efficient, and cost-effective construction solutions that fulfill the needs of clients while adhering to the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.
